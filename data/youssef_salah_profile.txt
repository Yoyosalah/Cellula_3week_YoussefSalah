Implemented a custom Dynamic Window Approach (DWA)-based local planner in ROS using Python.

4) Lane-Following Sub-Goal ROS Node

Developed a ROS node that:
- Computes a sub-goal ahead of the robot
- Ensures alignment parallel to the lane
- Publishes the goal for downstream navigation modules
- Visualizes output in RViz

The system ensures that the robot always follows the lane structure in a stable and forward-facing orientation.
